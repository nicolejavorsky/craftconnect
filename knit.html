American Cancer Society

  • What is the American Cancer Society?
    The ACS is dedicated to helping persons who face cancer. They support research, patient services, early detection, treatment and education.
  • What can you do?
    The American Cancer Society provides, free of charge, crocheted stoma covers for head/neck cancer patients. We are in need of volunteers to crochet these and donate the crocheted stoma covers for these patients. We have a pattern that can be supplied to anyone interested in helping us. Please have any interested volunteers contact Nancy Price, and she will mail or email pattern directions.

Angels for Hope

  • What is Angels for Hope?
    Angels for Hope sends hand-crocheted angels and butterflies to those who are severely injured and/or chronically ill as well as their caregivers. The angels and butterflies are a reminder that those suffering are not alone, and that others care.
  • What can you do?
    Crochet and donate angels and butterflies. contact Cindy Gabner, and she will mail or email pattern directions.
